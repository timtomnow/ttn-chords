Authoring mode unlocks everything for maintaining the songbook — creating and
editing songs, tagging beats, rhythm patterns, custom chords, reports, and the
library managers. It's off by default so the everyday view stays focused on
playing.

## Steps

1. Open **Settings**.
2. Find the **Authoring** section.
3. Turn on **Admin / authoring mode**.

## What you'll see

Authoring controls appear across the app — **New** and **Import** on Songs,
**Edit** and **Tag beats** on a song, **New** on Setlists and Reports, and extra
library-management sections in Settings. Turn it off any time to return to the
clean everyday view.
