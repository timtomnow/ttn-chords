**⚠️ Reports are still in development and not yet production-ready. Printed output
may need manual checking before you hand it out.**

Reports print through your browser, so "Save as PDF" in the print dialog is how
you get a PDF file.

## Before you start

- Build the report first — see [Build a printable report](/help/create-a-report).

## Steps

1. Open the report and open its **Print** view.
2. Your browser's print dialog appears.
3. Choose your printer, or pick **Save as PDF** as the destination.
4. Check the page size and margins, then print or save.

## What you'll see

The pages render at full size with their headers/footers and page numbers. Page
numbers refer to the report's own pages. Because printing uses the browser, exact
results vary a little between browsers — preview before sharing.
