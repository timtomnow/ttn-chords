The performance view shows a song full-screen and free of clutter, with large
chords and lyrics and controls for transposing and zooming. It also keeps the
screen awake while you play.

## Before you start

- You need at least one song. If your library is empty, see
  [Add songs from the starter library](/help/add-songs-from-library).

## Steps

1. Open **Songs** and tap the song you want.
2. Tap **Perform**.
3. Use the bottom bar to adjust as you play:
   - **Transpose** − / + shifts the key without changing the saved song.
   - The zoom − / + changes the text size.
4. Tap a chord name to see its chord diagram.
5. Tap the **✕** at the top left to exit.

## What you'll see

The song fills the screen. A small green dot appears while the screen is being
kept awake. Changes you make here (transpose, zoom) only affect this session —
they don't change the saved song.
