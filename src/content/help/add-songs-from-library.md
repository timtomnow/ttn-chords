The app ships with one or more **bundles** — ready-made collections of songs.
Syncing a bundle copies its songs into your own library, where you can play and
edit them. Anything you already have is left alone unless you choose to overwrite
it.

## Steps

1. Open **Settings** and find **Starter library**.
2. Next to a bundle, tap **Sync**.
3. If some songs match ones you already have (same title and artist), a dialog
   asks what to do with each:
   - **Import as duplicate** — adds a copy with a numbered name (e.g. *Song_1*).
   - **Overwrite existing** — replaces your current version.
   - Use **Apply to all** to make one choice for every match at once.
4. Tap **Import**.

## What you'll see

The bundle's songs appear in **Songs**. A short summary reports how many were
added, duplicated, or overwritten. Re-syncing later only prompts again for
matches — your edits are never lost unless you overwrite.
