Tagging beats records exactly when each chord lands, which is what the
[Highway view](/help/highway-view) needs to scroll in time. You tag by playing
along once, then fine-tune on a grid.

## Before you start

- Turn on [authoring (admin) mode](/help/turn-on-admin-mode).
- If the song has more than one difficulty version, tag each version separately.

## Steps

1. Open a song and tap **Tag beats**.
2. Set the **Tempo**, then tap **Start**. A one-bar count-in plays.
3. On each beat a chord lands, tap the big button (or press **Space**, **B**, or
   **F**).
4. When you finish, fine-tune in the editor: drag a chord on the grid, **nudge**
   it earlier/later, adjust the snap (**1/4**, **1/8**, **1/16**), or insert and
   delete bars.
5. Tap save to store the timing.

## What you'll see

Each chord gets an exact onset. The song will now play in the **Highway** view,
and you can re-open Tag beats any time to adjust.
