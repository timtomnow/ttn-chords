The app ships with common chord shapes for guitar, ukulele, bass, and piano. When
you need a different tuning or a shape that isn't bundled, add your own instrument
or chord chart.

## Before you start

- Turn on [authoring (admin) mode](/help/turn-on-admin-mode).

## Steps

1. Open **Settings** and find **Instrument & chords**.
2. To add a tuning, under **Custom instruments** tap **Add** and enter the
   instrument's name and string tuning.
3. To add or override a shape, under **Custom chord charts** tap **Add** (it adds
   for your current instrument), enter the chord symbol, and set the frets per
   string in the editor.

## What you'll see

Custom instruments appear alongside the bundled ones in **My instrument**. A
custom chart replaces the bundled shape for that chord wherever it's shown.
