The chord names in a song are clickable and show a chord diagram. That diagram is
drawn for whichever instrument you pick here — guitar, ukulele, bass, or piano —
so everyone sees shapes for the instrument they actually play.

## Steps

1. Open **Settings**.
2. Find the **Instrument & chords** section.
3. Under **My instrument**, choose your instrument.

## What you'll see

Chord diagrams across the app — the chord strip on a song, the pop-up when you
tap a chord name, and chord charts in reports — now use your chosen instrument.
