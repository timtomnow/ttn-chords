The tuner listens through your device's microphone and shows the nearest note and
how sharp or flat you are. It can run chromatically or target the open strings of
your instrument.

## Before you start

- Allow microphone access when your browser asks.

## Steps

1. Open **Tools** and tap **Tuner**.
2. Choose a **Mode**: **Chromatic**, or your instrument so it targets that
   tuning's strings.
3. Pluck a string and read the note name and the in-tune indicator.
4. If needed, adjust the **A4** reference pitch with the **−** / **+** buttons
   (default 440 Hz).

## What you'll see

The detected note appears large, with a meter showing whether you're sharp or
flat. In an instrument mode, the target strings are listed so you can tune one at
a time.
