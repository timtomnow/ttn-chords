All your songs, setlists, reports, and settings live on your device. Export them
to a file to keep a backup or move to another device, and import to bring them
back.

## Before you start

- Importing a file **replaces** all current data. Export first if you want to keep
  what you have.

## Steps

1. Open **Settings** and find **Backup & data**.
2. To save a backup, tap **Export JSON** — a file downloads to your device.
3. To restore, tap **Import JSON (replace)** and choose a previously exported
   file.
4. If you use the cross-app backup utility, tap **Restore from ttn-backup**.

## What you'll see

Export produces a dated `.json` file. After an import or restore the app reloads
with the restored data. Older backups are upgraded automatically to the current
format on import.
