The performance screen offers a few ways to read the same song. Switch between
them at any time to suit the song and the room.

## The views

- **Scroll** — the full song, chords over lyrics, with optional auto-scroll whose
  speed follows the tempo.
- **Lyrics** — large centred lyrics only, with chords hidden — handy for singing.
- **Highway** — chords laid out left-to-right on a beat grid that scrolls in time
  with the music. See [Play along with the Highway view](/help/highway-view).

## Steps

1. Open a song and tap **Perform**.
2. Use the view menu at the top right to pick **Scroll**, **Lyrics**, or
   **Highway**.
3. For Scroll and Lyrics, use the play button and speed control in the bottom bar
   to auto-scroll.

## What you'll see

The song re-draws in the chosen view. The bottom bar only shows the controls that
view supports, and your choice is remembered for next time.
