Running a setlist walks you through its songs in order in the same performance
screen used for a single song, with next/previous controls so you can move
between them hands-free.

## Before you start

- You need a setlist with at least one song — see
  [Build a setlist](/help/create-a-setlist).

## Steps

1. Open **Setlists** and tap the setlist.
2. Tap **Run**.
3. Use the **◀ / ▶** buttons in the bottom bar to move between songs.
4. All the usual performance controls work per song — transpose, zoom, reading
   view, and the metronome.

## What you'll see

Each song opens in turn, applying that entry's transpose/capo overrides. The top
bar shows your position, e.g. *2 / 8*. If a song was deleted after being added,
that slot offers to skip.
